The Process

How It Works

Simple, transparent, and documented at every step. Your equipment, your schedule.

01
Submit your request

Fill out the booking form with your equipment choice, dates, and contact info. We respond within 24 hours to confirm availability.

02
Confirm and pay

Once confirmed, payment is collected via Stripe, Venmo, or cash on pickup. Your slot is held once payment is received.

03
Document at pickup

Before leaving, complete the required photo checklist documenting condition from all required angles. 6 photos required.

04
Return and close out

Return by your scheduled time and complete the return photo checklist. Rental closes when all photos are submitted.

Required Photo Documentation

All 6 photos required at pickup AND return. Protects both parties and ensures fair condition assessment.
